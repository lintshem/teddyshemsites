Dudi Girls High School

Aim high

Dudi Girls High School (DGHS) was started in the year 1910. It has since grown to be an Extra county school. The school is located in Homabay county Kenya near Oyugis Town on the Kisumu-Oyugis Highway.

Motto

Aim high

Vision

A Girl school of first choice in provision of holistic education.

Mission

By providing quality education for the development of the minds of the nation torch-bearers for sustainable development.

Departments in the school

We teach the 13 subjects in the school with form 1 and form 2 students taking 11 subjects and the upper forms taking at least 7 subjects.  

1

Languages

Deals with the English language and the Kiswahili language.
2

 Mathematics

Deals with Mathematics only.
3

Sciences

deals with the Sciences i.e Biology, Chemistry and physics
4

Humanities

Deals with Geography, History and Christian Religious Education (CRE)
5

Technicals

Deals with Business Studies, French, Agriculture and Home Science
