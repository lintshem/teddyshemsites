School Must Knows(FAQs)

What subjects are offered in the school?

The school offers a total of 13 subjects. Three compulsory English, Kiswahili and Mathematics. Together with 3 sciences Biology , Chemistry and Physics. In Addition with 3 humanities CRE, Geography, history and 4 technicals Business studies, Agriculture, french and Home Science.  

When are the visiting days?

The school official visiting days are on the first sunday of each month unless communicated on the notice board otherwise. Please Note that the visiting time start at 9.00 am and ends at 5.00 pm. 

What to carry for the visitings?

We allow parents/Guardians to carry for their children cooked food and any other requirement. Carried cooked food will not be allowed in the dormitories though. 

What can cause students to be expelled?

Extreme cases of indiscipline which may include acts as follows; fighting in school, theft and vandalism (intentional damage of school property ) 

How do i contact the school?

Contact links to the school can be found on the the school home page and on the bottom of all pages on this site. We waiting to hear from you.

What with the students portal?

The students portal provide the student and their parents/guardians with personal information about their children and also  capture the fee payment and exam results.
